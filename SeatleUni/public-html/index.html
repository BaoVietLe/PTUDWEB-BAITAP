About Seattle University

Seattle University, founded in 1891, is a Jesuit Catholic university located on 50 acres on Seattle's Capitol Hill. We provide a rigorous, challenging education that transforms students from diverse backgrounds into leaders who values, knowledge and skills necessary for the modern world.

One of just over 300 schools to rank in the top-tier of leading universities and one that offers range of undergraduate and master's degrees in the West. This university has had a rank for more than a decade.
