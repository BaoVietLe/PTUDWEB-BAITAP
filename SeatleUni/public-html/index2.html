About Seattle University

Seattle University, founded in 1891, is a Jesuit Catholic university located on 50 acres in Seattle's Capitol Hill neighborhood. 7,400 students are enrolled in undergraduate and graduate programs within eight schools and colleges.

One of just 28 Jesuit Catholic universities in the U.S., Seattle University continues to rank in the top 10 among universities that offer a full range of masters and undergraduate degrees in the West. The university has held a top-10 ranking for more than a decade.
